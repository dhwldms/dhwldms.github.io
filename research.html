Immunology

My research in immunology focuses on understanding the immune response mechanisms at the molecular and cellular levels. I explore the pathways that regulate immune cell activation, differentiation, and memory formation.

Metabolic Biology

I investigate the intersection between metabolic processes and immune functions. Understanding how metabolic changes influence immune responses could lead to better therapeutic approaches for diseases like cancer and autoimmune disorders.

Biomedical Applications

My work aims to apply fundamental research in immunology and metabolism to real-world biomedical challenges, particularly in the development of targeted therapies and innovative diagnostic tools for complex diseases.
