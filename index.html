01Index / About

A molecular immunologist completing an integrated M.S.–Ph.D. at Sungkyunkwan University — trained across innate immunity, metabolic disease, and cancer metabolism.

Across these fields one thread stays constant: a commitment to rigorous, evidence-based science. Over five years I have published as lead or co-first author of six SCI papers — thirteen SCI(E) total — across more than six therapeutic areas, built transgenic and knock-out mouse models from the ground up, and authored a 400-page standardized protocol manual that remains my department's official reference.

I work most happily where science meets communication: synthesizing scattered literature into clear, balanced evidence, and bridging conversations across labs, disciplines, and languages. Off the page — reading, ballet, and film photography.
